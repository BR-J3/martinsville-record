What This Is

This is a sourced, chapter-by-chapter public record of how Martinsville, Virginia's city government arrived at $10 million in unauthorized budget amendments, a forensic audit, multiple federal lawsuits, a mayor's suspension, a special prosecutor chain, and an active Virginia State Police investigation — and who did what, in what order, with what documented consequences.

Every claim in these chapters traces to a named document, a named source, or a public record. No editorializing. No accusations stated — the sequence says it. The public record does the work.

All source documents, case numbers, docket references, and exhibit citations are linked within each chapter. This archive is permanent and updated as the record develops. Contact: justinpressva@proton.me
